how purple squirrel works

  • search
    search for ambassadors

    Search by company or industry and discover outstanding Ambassadors who will help you get hired quickly. Ambassadors are employed by, but are not official representatives of their employeer.

  • calender
    schedule an engagement

    Choose a time and date that's right for you and submit your request using our secure and trusted platform. Ambassadors connect with you in their free time, not during their work hours.

  • message
    engage with insider

    Gain an edge with personalized interview advice, access to unlisted jobs, earn employee referrals and more. Ambassadors may only make referrals in accordance with their employeer's policy.
